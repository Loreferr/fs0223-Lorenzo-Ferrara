Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appteizer topped with sweet caramelized onions , tart cranberries and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Image: Sally Vegas

FEATURED IN

Fresh Cranberries recipes that go beyond sauce

Ingredients

For The Focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 4 cup extra virgin oil, plus more for the pan

For The Topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onion
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ouncies brie, cut into cubes
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Metod

  1. Make The Focaccia Dough

In a Medium sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixed with paddle attachment, mix togheter all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. You dont need to actual knead the dough, though i sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
